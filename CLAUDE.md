## Project Overview

This is a **content repository** (not a codebase) for a UGC (User Generated Content) webinar by Socials agency. It contains marketing materials in Czech and Slovak.

## Repository Structure

- `kontext.md` — Webinar brief (Czech). Defines target audience, key takeaways, tone of voice, and content boundaries for the webinar "UGC pro výkonnostní marketing"
- `playbook.md` — Full UGC playbook (Slovak). Contains strategy (for founders/CMO), execution framework (for marketing teams), brief templates, outreach message templates, pricing guide, and internal feedback notes
- `socials-ready-doc.pdf` — Supporting PDF document

## Key Context

- **Agency:** Socials — a performance marketing boutique
- **Webinar name (working title):** "UGC videa: Kedy UGC reálne zarába peniaze a kedy je to len drahý obsah"
- **Date:** Wednesday 8.4.2026, 10:00
- **Presentation:** Canva
- **Webinar platform:** Riverside (https://riverside.com/use-cases/webinars)
- **Webinar topic:** How to turn UGC into a repeatable system that reduces PNO (cost ratio) and increases campaign profitability
- **Target audience:** Performance marketers, CMOs, and e-shop owners spending 100k+ CZK/month on paid social
- **Languages:** Content is in Czech (`kontext.md`) and Slovak (`playbook.md`)

### Business Goal
- **Primary goal:** Acquire new clients for Socials agency
- **Positioning:** Establish Socials as the go-to agency for UGC creatives (both human creators and AI avatars)
- **Differentiator:** Socials uses AI avatars alongside traditional UGC creators in Meta Ads — presentation includes real results/data from these campaigns
- **Bonus/lead magnet:** Attendees who watch until the end receive the UGC playbook as a reward (retention incentive)

## Content Guidelines (from kontext.md)

- Data-first, no fluff — every claim backed by real campaign numbers
- Senior-level tone: direct, pragmatic, no marketing clichés
- Show what didn't work alongside what did
- No generic "what is UGC" content — audience already knows the basics
- No sales pitch for Socials services — webinar must stand on its own as value

## Playbook Structure (from playbook.md)

The playbook is split into two parts per internal feedback:
1. **Strategy (Founder/CMO)** — When UGC makes sense, psychology of video sales, creative testing decisions, budget/ROI
2. **Execution (Marketing team)** — 15-step collaboration checklist, UGC brief template, outreach message templates, pricing guide, performance benchmarks (CTR, CPA, ROAS, watch time)
